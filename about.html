About Care Hospitals, Srilanka

Sri Lanka We ensure you receive the best possible care throughout your recovery journey.





Care Hospitals,Established by Dr. Pradeep C. Silva in 1988, Care Hospitals in Sri Lanka has become a symbol of excellence in healthcare. With a robust presence across the healthcare ecosystem, Care Hospitals offers a comprehensive range of services, from routine wellness and preventive care to cutting-edge life-saving treatments and advanced diagnostic services. Over the years, the institution has served more than 22 million patients from over 150 countries, earning its place as a trusted global healthcare provider.

Guided by Dr. Silva's visionary leadership, Care Hospitals has remained at the forefront of medical advancements in Sri Lanka. The hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by expert medical professionals dedicated to providing compassionate and personalized care. Through innovations in minimally invasive surgeries, advanced diagnostics, and precision medicine, Care Hospitals ensures that world-class healthcare is accessible to everyone. Beyond clinical care, Care Hospitals is actively involved in medical research and education. The institution's training programs aim to develop the next generation of healthcare professionals, while its research initiatives focus on improving patient outcomes and advancing medical science. By prioritizing quality and patient care, Care Hospitals has not only transformed healthcare delivery in Sri Lanka but has also positioned the country as a key destination for global medical tourism. Care Hospitals remains committed to its mission of delivering international-standard healthcare to every individual, leading with compassion, expertise, and innovation.
